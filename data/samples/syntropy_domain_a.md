Title: Solid-State Battery Interfaces
Authors: Sample Team

We study lithium metal anodes with ceramic electrolytes for solid-state batteries.
Key challenges include dendrite suppression, interfacial impedance, and mechanical stress.
We review thin-film coatings (LiNbO3, Al2O3), stack pressure effects, and ion transport pathways.
We propose nanoscale interlayers to stabilize the interface and reduce impedance growth.
