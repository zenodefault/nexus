Title: Fast Vision Transformer for Low-Data Settings
Authors: Sample Team

Abstract
We propose a compact vision transformer for low-data classification. On a 10-class dataset,
our method improves accuracy from 78.0% to 92.0% while reducing training time by 30%.
We also report a 0.12 decrease in calibration error compared to the baseline.

Methods
We train a 6-layer transformer with patch size 8 and use strong augmentation. The baseline
is a small CNN with the same parameter count. We evaluate for 50 epochs with early stopping.

Results
Accuracy (top-1) baseline: 78.0%
Accuracy (top-1) proposed: 89.0%
Training time reduction: 25%
Calibration error reduction: 0.05

Conclusion
The proposed model improves accuracy and reduces training time under small data regimes.
