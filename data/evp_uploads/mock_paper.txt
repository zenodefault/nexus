Title: Lightweight MRI Classification with Efficient CNNs
Authors: A. Patel, B. Kim, C. Lopez

We study brain MRI classification using compact CNN architectures designed for low compute settings.
The approach uses transfer learning on a small labeled dataset and applies aggressive data augmentation.
Key results show a 2-3% accuracy improvement over a standard baseline with reduced training time.
Limitations include sensitivity to preprocessing and limited generalization to rare tumor classes.
We suggest exploring contrastive pretraining and multi-scale feature fusion for further gains.
